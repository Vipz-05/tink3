Delicious Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken (or any preferred meat)
  • 1 large Onion (thinly sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Spices: Cinnamon, Cardamom, Cloves, Bay Leaf
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Cilantro and Mint for garnishing
  • 2 tbsp Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • 1/4 cup Fried Onions

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and soak it for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, heat ghee and saut√© the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for another minute. Then add tomatoes and spices.
  4. Mix in the chicken (or meat of choice) and cook until browned. Add yogurt and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate pot, boil water, add salt, and cook rice until 75% done.
  6. Layer the cooked rice over the meat mixture. Add fried onions and fresh cilantro on top.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to combine.
  8. Garnish with fresh mint and serve hot!
